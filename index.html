How it works

Our Mission

We are a waste management startup. We work with schools, colleges, institutions & such to micro-manage their waste. And, we reward their efforts with recycled stationery, workshops, sharing drives and much more. We currently deal with paper waste only.

Our mission is to empower people so that they can handle their waste more responsibly and much more easily.

Workshops

We regularly conduct workshops in schools and organisations to educate and sensitize people about waste management practices. Workshops can be availed against green points as a part of waste management plan or can be requested separately.
