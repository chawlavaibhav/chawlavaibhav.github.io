Waste is worth more.

Give us your waste and we will give you cleaner environment, green stationary and so much more!

How it works

We make a waste management plan with you
We pick up waste as per your schedule
In return, you get green stationary and workshops
Plus, you make the planet a little more green!

Our mission

Our mission is to empower people so that they can handle their waste more responsibly and much more easily.

We work with schools, colleges, institutions & such to micro-manage their waste. And, we reward their efforts with recycled stationery, workshops, sharing drives and much more. We currently deal with paper waste only.

Workshops

We regularly conduct workshops in schools and organisations to educate and sensitize people about waste management practices. Workshops can be availed against green points as a part of waste management plan or can be requested separately.
